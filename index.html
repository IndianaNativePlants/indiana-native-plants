Landscaping with Native Plants

Indiana Native Plant Recommendations for Home Landscapes

Why Use Native Plants?

Native plants are adapted to Indiana’s climate and soils. They require less maintenance, provide essential wildlife habitat, and help preserve biodiversity.

Woods Edge (Partial Shade)

Flowers

  • Amsonia tabernaemontana
  • Campanulastrum americanum
  • Eupatorium purpureum
  • Iris cristata
  • Lobelia siphilitica
  • Maianthemum racemosum
  • Mertensia virginica
  • Monarda fistulosa

Grasses

  • Chasmanthium latifolium

Shrubs & Trees

  • Cornus drummondii
  • Hamamelis virginiana

Woodland (Shade)

Ferns

  • Adiantum pedatum
  • Matteuccia struthiopteris
  • Osmunda claytoniana

Flowers

  • Aquilegia canadensis
  • Asarum canadense
  • Polygonatum biflorum
  • Trillium grandiflorum

Flowers

  • Allium cernuum

  • Echinacea purpurea
  • Liatris spicata
  • Rudbeckia hirta

Grasses

  • Andropogon gerardii
  • Panicum virgatum
  • Sorghastrum nutans
